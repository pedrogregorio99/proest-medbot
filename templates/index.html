Programming Languages

What is a Chatbot?

Google says ...

A Chatbot is a computer program designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the Internet.

Does the Chatbot store my data?

Well yes, but actually no.

Don't worry, it doesn't. The only thing it saves is the question you ask him, whenever he doesn't know how to answer it.

What is Medbot?

Your new best friend!

Medbot is a system developed in order to help you solve a certain symptom you might have. You tell it the problem and he'll try to give you an amazing solution to it!

How does the bot know what to answer?

MedBot is quite smart!

And he'll look to the models he has, compares to what you sent him and finds a proper answer. When it comes to the diseases, MedBot has it's own database and he looks for special words in your message. This way he'll find what you want in a faster way and with less chance to errors.
